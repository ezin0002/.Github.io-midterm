The Life of a Starfish

Starfish are amazing sea creatures. They can be found in oceans all over the world, moving slowly on the ocean floor. Even though they do not have a brain or blood, they survive using their water-based system. They eat small sea animals and can regrow their arms if they lose one. These unique abilities make starfish very special.

Interesting Starfish Facts

  • Starfish have the ability to grow back lost arms.
  • They move using hundreds of tiny feet on their arms.
  • There are more than 2,000 species of starfish in the world.
  • Some starfish can split in half and grow into two separate starfish.
